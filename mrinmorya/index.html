"PROMISE OF A YOUNG MIND ? "

I am a communication design student from India. Who is greatly fascinated by stories everywhere, in all forms, and is on her way to experience, learn, and harness the qualities of a good storyteller. This process has led me to explore areas like writing (prose and poetry), illustrating, designing publications, photography (various kinds), experience design, branding, etc. And all my experiences till now have reassured my belief in the omnipresent nature of stories. Therefore, I will always try to be a communicator who always attempts at adding that storyteller charm to all her projects. And, that is the promise of this young mind.
